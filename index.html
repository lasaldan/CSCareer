Lessons Learned

Lesson: Build Trust

  • Trusting your team is One of the most valuable benefits to be had.
  • I know that my coworkers have my back. If something were to happen to me, my family would be taken care of.
  • Takes a long time to build trust - faster if you have hardship together. We went through a lot together to get to where we are.
  • When a wife had mountains of medical bills, we paid them off.
  • Work to develop trust with your future coworkers.

Lesson: Be Honest

  • Not just with clients
  • We strived to be transparent so our clients wouldn't feel taken advantage of.
  • Honesty has paid off in the long run. We've turned clients away when they didn't need us
  • Honest with Coworkers
  • If something is bothering you in a workplace, face the issue. Don't just let it linger

Lesson: Manage Expectations

  • Communication Most important for success in any field
  • Know what's expected of you, and that the client/enduser knows what's expected of them.
  • Keep communications open when things change on either side.
  • Be a problem solver and a good partner, you'll become very valuable in whatever field

Lesson: Never Stop Learning

  • Every field can use some innovation
  • Notice pain points and get creative
